# Our Clients

## Who we work with

Our clients are DTC and SaaS companies, typically with established revenue, who need performance marketing to be more systematic and more effective. They usually have some marketing in place already — paid ads, email, maybe a team member or two — and they're trying to make it work better.

The clients we do well with have access to real data, can make decisions without five layers of approval, and are willing to hear uncomfortable truths about what's working and what isn't.

## Who we don't work with

We don't take clients where the core problem is the product or the offer. If the thing they're selling doesn't work well enough to retain customers, better marketing is the wrong solution.

We don't work with clients who want us to just execute tactics without context or strategy. That produces bad work and bad outcomes.

We don't work with clients who need a vendor to manage, rather than a partner to work with.

## What clients can expect from us

Honest assessments. If something isn't working, we say so and explain why. We don't keep running a campaign that's underperforming because it's easier than having the conversation.

Clear communication. Updates are written, decisions are documented, and there's always a next step and an owner.

Access to the people doing the work. Clients talk directly to the people running their accounts, not to an account manager relaying messages.

## What we expect from clients

Timely access to data, accounts, and decision-makers. When we need something to keep work moving, we need it within a day or two, not a week.

Honest context about what's happening on their side — product changes, internal constraints, things that might affect the work.

Decisions that actually get made. We can provide recommendations all day, but if the client can't make a call, nothing moves.
