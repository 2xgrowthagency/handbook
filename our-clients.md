# Our Clients

We do our best work with businesses that want a real growth partner — not a vendor they can hand tasks to without context or accountability.

The ideal 2x client has something worth scaling: a credible offer, a product that delivers, and a leadership team that's willing to hear the truth. They don't need to have everything figured out. They do need to care about substance over appearances.

---

## Who we work well with

Strong-fit clients understand that growth usually comes from sharpening the offer, improving the message, fixing friction in the funnel, and then scaling what works — in that order.

They're willing to invest in the basics: access to data, access to decision-makers, reasonable budgets, and enough internal alignment to actually act on good recommendations. They don't expect paid media to rescue a broken product, confused positioning, or bad retention. They want a partner who'll think critically, challenge assumptions, and help them make better decisions across the funnel.

---

## Who we're not a fit for

We're not right for everyone, and saying that early saves everyone time.

We don't work well with clients who want guaranteed outcomes on unrealistic timelines, who chase tactics before understanding the underlying problem, or who treat agencies like order takers. We're not here to defend vanity metrics, manufacture certainty where none exists, or participate in performative urgency every time a number moves the wrong direction for a day.

If you want constant availability, endless check-ins, vague feedback loops, or a team that'll say yes to keep the contract comfortable — we're the wrong shop.

---

## What a great partnership looks like

Direct, calm, high-trust.

Both sides are honest about goals, constraints, and what success should actually mean. Feedback is specific. Decisions get made. Access isn't blocked by layers of internal politics or slow handoffs.

There's room for disagreement without drama, because everyone is trying to get to the best answer — not win points.

We bring strategy, execution, and pattern recognition. Clients bring business context, fast decisions, and a real commitment to follow-through. When that exchange is working, momentum compounds fast.

---

## What we expect from each other

From clients: clarity on priorities, timely communication, access to the people and systems we need to do the work, and willingness to engage with hard truths when they show up.

From us: honest recommendations, high standards, clean execution, and clear communication about progress, risks, and next steps. We won't pretend something is working when it's not.

In return, we expect clients not to hide context, delay decisions, or ask for shortcuts that undermine the result. The best partnerships are built on candor, responsiveness, and shared accountability for the outcome.
