# Who We Are

2x exists to help businesses grow with clear strategy, disciplined execution, and marketing that holds up over time. We are not interested in vanity metrics, busywork, or short-lived wins that look good in a slide deck and collapse a quarter later. Our mission is simple: build growth systems that are useful, honest, and sustainable for the people we work with.

The agency is shaped by the way John and Jenn work. John comes from an operator's mindset built through teaching, building, and learning the hard way. He values self-awareness over ego, and he would rather tell the truth than protect a bad idea with polite language. Jenn brings the execution engine: performance marketing strategy, creative direction, retention, and the internal systems that let a team deliver consistently. Together, they cover strategy, operations, and hands-on marketing. That balance is the point.

Our origin story is less about a dramatic founding moment and more about a shared standard. John and Jenn built 2x around the belief that good marketing should be grounded in fundamentals, not trend-chasing. They each saw how often businesses get pushed toward tactics before anyone gets clear on positioning, customer understanding, or what success is actually supposed to look like. 2x was built as an answer to that. Start with the truth. Build the system. Then scale what works.

What we believe about marketing is straightforward. Strategy comes first. Execution matters. Operations matter too. A strong campaign can only carry so much weight if the offer is weak, the message is fuzzy, or the follow-through is a mess. We believe in performance marketing, but not in treating paid media like magic. Paid search, paid social, conversion optimization, landing pages, email, and SMS all work better when they are connected to a real point of view and a disciplined process.

We also believe sustainable growth beats short-term theatrics. Community matters. Trust matters. Retention matters. The goal is not to squeeze every last click out of a campaign and call it a win. The goal is to help brands create momentum they can keep. That means making smart decisions, measuring what matters, and being willing to say, "That's not going to work. Here's what will."

John leads client strategy, positioning, discovery, and new business development. He brings clarity, sharp thinking, and a bias toward honest conversations. Jenn is Co-Founder and COO, leading internal systems and people while also driving performance marketing strategy, creative direction, and retention. She is a builder of both campaigns and teams, with deep experience across DTC, SaaS, PR, events, and community-led growth.

We're not trying to be everything to everyone. We're here to be the kind of partner that makes the work better, the thinking sharper, and the growth more durable.
